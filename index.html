About Us

Our new hotel – ASHOKA INN has been opened on 28th Jan 2021. The ASHOKA INN hotel is a classic hotel located in the heart of the City. The hotel is walking distance from Railway Station and Bus Station. The beautiful layout and architecture gives the hotel a very special atmosphere. The Ashoka INN hotel has 10 beautiful and lavish Rooms with full ventilation and is today an attractive accommodation with all modern facilities. There is also our one more hotel named Ashoka Dining Hall for refreshment which is walking distance from this hotel. The hotel offers room service (limited hours), Business Center and much more… We strongly believe that “Cleanliness is next to Godliness” so we have neat and clean areas. As we know that today’s situation is of Covid so sanitization is our first priority. We give importance to day to day sanitization. We sanitize each and every part of the hotel. We maintain proper sanitization and hygienic place to live in. We provide complimentary breakfast to our customers and laundry service is also been taken care of. We try to provide better hospitality to our customers. We are available for our customers anytime and every time. We try our best to provide better services for our customers.
